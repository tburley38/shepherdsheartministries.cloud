Our mission

Meeting people where they are, with the love of Christ.

Shepherds Heart Ministries exists to serve people through faithful presence, compassionate listening, prayer, and encouragement.

We honor every person as a whole, embodied soul—with physical, emotional, relational, and spiritual needs—and seek to bring calm, dignity, and hope without judgment.

Chaplaincy services

Care for the whole person.

Support is offered with kindness and respect, wherever you are on your spiritual journey.

Compassionate Presence

A steady, caring presence during illness, grief, transition, loneliness, or seasons of uncertainty.

Spiritual Care

Prayer, Scripture, encouragement, and respectful conversation centered on each person’s needs.

Listening & Support

A confidential place to be heard, find hope, and take the next step with dignity and compassion.

You are not alone.

No matter what you are facing today, there is hope. Your life has value, you are deeply loved by God, and you do not have to walk through this alone.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or thoughts of suicide, please seek immediate help using the resources below.
